# Research Dashboard Demo Brief

Build a chart-led research dashboard that translates the OCMacro dashboard case into a local, reusable fixture without copying outside assets, data, labels, or brand marks.

## Required Pattern

- Narrow global chrome with brand, navigation, and compact utilities.
- Wide content area with stacked analytical cards.
- First screen leads with conclusion, headline metric, and the TACO pressure index chart.
- Each major block uses eyebrow, title, summary, chart or feed, and source/footer rhythm.
- Event notes use threat, drawdown, and meaning so policy or market moves become explainable.
- Mobile keeps the same hierarchy and prioritizes the first chart and conclusion.

## Required States

- Loading pressure feed
- No live events in the selected window
- Pressure feed sync failed

## Constraints

- Use synthetic content only.
- Do not copy external screenshots, chart data, brand marks, or source wording.
- Keep card and panel radius at 8px or less.
- Include hover, focus, active, disabled, loading, empty, and error evidence for QA.
